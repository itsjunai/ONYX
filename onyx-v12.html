DATA USE POLICY — ONYX PLATFORM MUST READ BEFORE ACCESS

1. Purpose & Scope

ONYX is a closed-loop intelligence aggregation platform designed exclusively for authorised personnel of OECS member states, IMPACS, and designated national security ministries. This platform aggregates publicly available information from open-source news and government websites for intelligence analysis purposes only.

2. Data Sources & Legal Basis

All data ingested by this platform is sourced from publicly accessible web pages and RSS feeds. ONYX does not access, store, or process any classified, private, or personally identifiable information beyond what is already in the public domain. Aggregation is conducted under the fair dealing provisions applicable in OECS jurisdictions for purposes of news monitoring and security analysis.

  • Sources are limited to public government portals, registered news organisations, and official law enforcement communications
  • No login-protected, paywalled, or restricted content is accessed
  • Data is processed locally and is not transmitted to any third-party server
  • Retention is capped at 60 signals per region per session

3. Operator Obligations

By accessing this platform, the operator agrees to:

  • Use aggregated intelligence solely for authorised national security, law enforcement, and governance purposes
  • Not redistribute, publish, or share raw feed data outside the authorised agency without written approval
  • Treat all CRIT and WARN level signals as unverified until corroborated by a second source
  • Report any suspected data integrity issues to the platform administrator immediately

4. Proprietary Notice

The ONYX Threat Score Engine™, Sovereign Scrubber™, Caribbean Node Manifest™, and all associated algorithms are proprietary trade secrets. Reverse engineering, copying, or redistribution of any component of this platform is prohibited under applicable intellectual property law.

5. No Warranty

Intelligence signals are derived from open-source feeds and carry no guarantee of accuracy, completeness, or timeliness. Operators must apply independent analytical judgement before acting on any signal.
